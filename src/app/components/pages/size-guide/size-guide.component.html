How to Measure

1

Bust/Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust/chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

2

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, the narrowest part of your torso.

3

Hip

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, typically 7-8 inches below your waistline.

Size Guide Tips

  • •

    Use a soft measuring tape for accurate measurements.

  • •

    Keep the measuring tape snug but not tight against your body.

  • •

    For the most accurate fit, have someone else take your measurements.

  • •

    If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.
